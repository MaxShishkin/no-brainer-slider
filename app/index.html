No Brainer Slider

A really simple css3 drop-in slider

Download or Contribute

This is a small css-driven slider with really simplistic javascript logic. The goal was to make a simple drop-in solution, that can be used standalone or as a blueprint for more complex work on top. Also it's a good fit to use in prototyping.
